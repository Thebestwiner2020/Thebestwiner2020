Piggy is a free-to-play survival horror story game released in January 21st 2020 on ROBLOX by MiniToon.

The objective of the game is to escape the map by finding and using keys, tools, and more, all while avoiding an evil anthropomorphic Infected animal.

It won multiple Bloxy Awards during the 8th Annual Bloxy Awards, including Game Of The Year.
